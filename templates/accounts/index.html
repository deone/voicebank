About Us

Nigerian Voice Bank is Nigeria's first online voice casting tool.

The Nigerian Voice Bank is an initiative of Flame Media Works and have been designed to cater for advertising agencies, theatrical producers, casting agents, and radio and television producers who require access to an audio database of Nigeria's best voices.

The Nigerian Voice Bank desires to create an opportunity for every creative voice talent in Nigeria.

The Nigerian Voice Bank is a total voice casting tool where users can easily search for artists using our simplified and direct search options. Every voice on the voice bank is uploaded under a specific category. From the search results, users can listen to the artist's voice clips or download the clips. Users can also contact voice talent directly and book the artist for the recording if they pick an interest on anyone.
