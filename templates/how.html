Registering On the Voice Bank

STEP 1

The first step of registering your voice on the voice bank is to join by clicking the join link on the top right corner of the site.

This allows you to generate a username and password, using your already existing email account and a new password.

Go to http://www.nigerianvoicebank.com
Click on Join.
Complete the registration form and send.

STEP 2

Proceed to update your profile and and upload your profile picture. Remember to provide your correct contact information.

STEP 3

The next step will be to upload your voice clip if you already have them. You may choose to record your voice demo at our studio. To get more information, click here

Recording Your Voice Demo

You can record your voice demo at any studio of your choice and upload on our websites. Your voice demo must be in MP3 format and not more than 60 seconds.

Recording At Our Studio

To book your recording, send a mail to customercare@nigerianvoicebank.com with your name, phone number, location and the word VOICE RECORDING. You can also send us an SMS or just call our customer care lines on 08092796787 to book a session.

Joining the Nigerian Voice Bank is free. This includes the hosting of your voice demos.

You can upload as many as 5 voice demos on the voice bank.

Please contact 08092796787 or customercare@nigerianvoicebank.com for assistance.

Recording your voice demo at our studio will cost you 5000 Naira although terms and conditions apply.

Terms & Conditions
