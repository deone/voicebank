Renewals

Subscription on the Nigerian Voice Bank is valid for 1 year from the day your Voice clips is uploaded to the website.

Notification will be sent to you periodically via email or SMS with instructions on how to renew your subscription.

If you fail to renew your subscription, you will receive an email on the expiry date informing you that your subscription has expired. Your account will then be deleted from the website.

The cost to renew your subscription for another year is just 1000 Naira. The renewal fee allows you to keep your existing clips on the website for another year.

If you would like to replace your existing clips that will be done for 1000 Naira per clip.
