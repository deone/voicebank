    About Us

    NVB Production

    Nigerian Voice Bank is Nigeria's first online voice casting tool.

    The Nigerian Voice Bank is an initiative of Flame Media Works and have been designed to cater for advertising agencies, theatrical producers, casting agents, and radio and television producers who require access to an audio database of Nigeria's best voices.

    The Nigerian Voice Bank desires to create an opportunity for every creative voice talent in Nigeria.

    The Nigerian Voice Bank is a total voice casting tool where users can easily search for artists using our simplified and direct search options. Every voice on the voice bank is uploaded under a specific category. From the search results, users can listen to the artist's voice clips or download the clips. Users can also contact voice talent directly and book the artist for the recording if they pick an interest on anyone.

    Artists

    Imagine being contacted for a job you never knew existed and never applied for just because someone heard your voice on the voice bank and liked it. That's the miracle of having your voice on the Nigerian voice bank. If you are a voice artist, getting your voice on the Nigerian voice bank is the best way to market your talent. Whether you are an independent artist or a managed artist, the Nigerian Voice Bank offers you your own page on the site to showcase your talent.

    Vision

    Our vision is to create a platform where all Nigerian and African voice talents can be heard and equally connect these talents to the best production jobs.

    To inspire professionalism in the Nigerian voice artistry.

    Mission

    • To create a resourceful and engaging online platform.
    • To simplify the process of contacting a voice talent using creative and highly innovative strategies.

    Core Values

    • Service Delivery
