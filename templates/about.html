    About Us

    We don’t just pride in the fact that we are Nigeria’s first online voice casting site, we are more. We are a team of creatives, giving life to every script and adding value to every company that collides with us. Our talents are professionals in the act, delivering excellent voice over with the right accents.

    We go beyond owning the largest online voice over community in Nigeria to being a world class audio Production Company based in Lagos. Our approach to work is global and that is evident in our client base that spans almost every continent in the world with countries such as USA, UK, UAE, Singapore, Lebanon, France and South Africa.

    From our creative Den in Lagos we have left our voice prints on brands like Milo, Xoom, HP, Dettol, GE, Panadol, Glo Caf Awards to mention a few. We produce, translate, copy write, localize and even lip sync. That’s who we are. So, how can we be of help to you.

    NVB Production

    Nigerian Voice Bank Production (NVB PRODUCTION) is a world class audio production company operating in Lagos Nigeria with clients from over 10 countries including USA, UK, South Africa, UAE, Singapore, Lebanon, India etc.

    Working with us is simple, just post your job which is essentially your brief, select your preferred voice or request for samples, select a talent then book a session which is the preferred date and time you will like to record.

    You can listen in and direct the session via skype and have the dry voice sent to you immediately. Our projects are concluded within 24 to 48 hours.

    Our Production capability includes voice overs, Jingle Production, IVR, Audio books, Animation, explainer videos etc. we also do Lip Syncing, translation and copywriting.

    Feel free to request a quote today.
