What to bring to the recording

The voice artist should come to the recording with their scripts. These scripts should have been properly rehearsed before recording day.

Remember each voice clip requires a script. Apparently, if you are only recording 2 clips then you only need 2 scripts.

The Nigerian Voice bank will not supply scripts!

All scripts are to be delivered within 60 seconds. Scripts should be original or reworked, using fictitious brand names and company names. Voice artists should respect copyright laws.

The Nigerian Voice Bank will not take responsibility for any legal issues emanating from content supplied by any artist.

Please ensure that you are prepared for the recording and that you have rehearsed your scripts!

If for any reason the Nigerian Voice Bank considers your voice clip not suitable or too inexperienced, the Nigerian voice bank reserves the right to decline your subscription. In this case your subscription fee will be refunded.
