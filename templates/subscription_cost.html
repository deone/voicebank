Voice Bank Subscription Cost

Now that you have registered on the Voice Bank the next step is to subscribe for a plan. There are three subscription plans on the voice bank:

  • Premium Subscription
  • General Subscription
  • Kids Subscription

To get your voice on the site, you may choose from the following plans.

Subscription Plan Benefits Amount (Naira)
Premium (Artist 13 years and above) One year Subscription with the recording and hosting of four voice clips. Maximum length for each clip is 60secs. 4000
General (Artist 13 years and above) One year Subscription with the recording and hosting of two voice clips. Maximum length for each clip is 60secs. 3000
Kids (Artist below 13) One year subscription with the recording and hosting of 2 voice clip. Maximum length for each clip is 60secs. 2500
Additional Subscription

You can host up to ten voice clips. Each additional voice clip, attracts an extra cost of one thousand naira each (#1,000) All the above include:

  • Hosting of your voice on the site for one year.
  • The recording of your voice clips in a professional studio except for General subscription
  • Promotion of your voice in our most recent artist section only for full subscription
  • Your own page and address on the site.
  • Your picture, biography and profile on your page.
