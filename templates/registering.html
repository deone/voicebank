Registering On the Voice Bank

The first step to getting your voice on the voice bank is to join, by clicking the join link on the top corner of the site. Generate a username and password, using your already existing email account and a new set of password.

After which you create your profile and upload your profile picture. The next step will be to upload your voice clip if you already have them and if you don’t, then you need to book for your recording. To book for your recording send a mail to customercare@nigerianvoicebank.com with your name, phone number, location and the word VOICE RECORDING. You can also send us a text or just call our customer care lines on 08092796787 or 07044700254. After recording you upload your voice clip. You can have as many as 10 voice clips. Note: all voice clips should be in MP3 format and max of 60seconds long. “YOUR VOICE CLIPS WILL BE ACTIVATED ON THE SITE AFTER YOU HAVE PAID FOR YOUR SUBSCRIPTION” When filling your profile,Your phone number and email address is very important. Ensure they are correct because you will be contacted directly for job offers Complete the registration process then click on register. You will receive an email or sms or both prompting you with information on how to activate your profile. Follow this instruction precisely to effectively activate your profile. Please contact customercare@nigerianvoicebank.com for assistance
