Registering On the Voice Bank

STEP 1

The first step of registering your voice on the voice bank is to join by clicking the join link on the top right corner of the site.

This allows you to generate a username and password, using your already existing email account and a new set of password.

  • Go to http://www.nigerianvoicebank.com
  • Click on Join
  • Complete the registration form
After completing the registration form you click on join.

STEP 2

Proceed to update your profile and and upload your profile picture. Remember to provide your correct contact informations.

STEP 3

The next step will be to upload your voice clip if you already have them and if you don’t, then you need to book for your recording.

STEP 4

To book for your recording send a mail to customercare@nigerianvoicebank.com with your name, phone number, location and the word VOICE RECORDING. You can also send us a text or just call our customer care lines on 08092796787 or 07044700254.
Note: All voice clips should be in MP3 format and max of 60 seconds long.

STEP 5

Before your voice clips or showcase clips can be recorded at our studios, you must have paid for your subscription. Your voice clips will not be activated on the site until your subscriptions have been received.
You can upload as many as 10 voice clips or showcase clips on the voice bank.

“YOUR VOICE CLIPS WILL BE ACTIVATED ON THE SITE AFTER YOU HAVE PAID FOR YOUR SUBSCRIPTION”
